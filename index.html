There are always choices.

And a lot of them...

You can try to buy a ready-made theme (there are lots of places).

Something usually called "multifunctional", "multipurpose", "ready-for-everything" and suchlike.

Looks like pretty cool way. You see stunning demo, dozens of sliders, hundreds of animations and effects. You read an endless lists of options. Mind-blowing, and you shout "I want it immediately!!!". That's what is targeted. This is just a marketing.

But let's take a look under the hood. All these cool-looking stuff actually scripts and software modules. Think how much of them You will actually use. Have thought? Excellent. Everything else - is overweight. Yes exactly! This is "fat ", which makes Your site unable to run fast.

Go further. All demos invented by a really cool designers and marketers. To get something like - You have to hire these guys too.

And further. Theme options panel - real challenge. Hundreds pages of user's manual. A large number of required plug-ins and widgets etc. Often, people are forced to hire freelancers to handle with their "multipurpose" themes. And still not getting what to expect when admiring demo. All this can be called something like "Theme-fighting". And actually it is.

Is that all? Actually, no. Also support, extensions, updates, safety and a lot more. But so much of the text... If you want details - ask "Google".

Summarize? The choice is always and only Yours. Let the mind chooses, not feelings.
